Technical Architecture

The AI Dharma Snippet Generator employs a sophisticated RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) architecture that combines the power of large language models with a specialized knowledge base of the Bhagavad Gita. When a user poses a question, the system first converts it into vector embeddings and searches through a vector database containing indexed verses and their semantic meanings.

The most relevant shlokas are retrieved and passed as context to the LLM, which then generates a spiritually-aligned response that incorporates the ancient wisdom with modern language understanding. This approach ensures authenticity while maintaining the flexibility and conversational nature of contemporary AI systems.

The system runs locally using Ollama, ensuring privacy and fast response times, while maintaining the ability to scale with cloud-based deployments when needed.
